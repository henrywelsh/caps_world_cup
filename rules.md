# World Cup Shared-Ownership Pool – Rules & Explanation

Welcome to the World Cup Shared-Ownership Pool! This page explains **how the auction works, how ownership is calculated, and what you need to know to participate safely**.

---

## 1. Overview

This is a **shared-ownership auction** for World Cup teams.  

- You purchase **caps** to own a percentage of one or more teams.  
- **Ownership percentage** determines your payout as teams advance.  
- **Early bids are slightly more valuable than later bids** via a time-based multiplier.  
- **All bids are final.** There are no refunds or bid reversals.

---

## 2. How Bidding Works

1. **Bid Placement**
   - Select a team.
   - Enter the number of caps to bid.
   - Confirm the bid.
   - Your bid is **applied immediately**, if within allowed limits.

2. **Time-Weighted Caps**
   - Each bid receives a **multiplier** based on when it is placed.
   - Early bids = higher weighted value.
   - Weighted caps determine ownership, **not raw caps**.

| Time Period       | Multiplier |
|------------------|------------|
| First 5 days      | 1.15x      |
| Next 5 days       | 1.08x      |
| Final period      | 1.00x      |

**Example:**  
- Bid 100 caps in the first 5 days → counts as **115 weighted caps**.  
- Bid 100 caps in the final period → counts as **100 weighted caps**.  

3. **Ownership Calculation**
ownership % = weighted_caps / total_weighted_caps_for_team


**Important:** Ownership **can change at any time** as new bids are placed.

---

## 3. Payment Rules

- Users may **only bid up to $50** worth of caps until they submit payment via Venmo.  
- Admin must **mark you as paid** to unlock additional bidding.  
- Bids **exceeding your current allowed limit are blocked**.  
- **Unpaid bids will be voided**, and payouts withheld until payment is received.

> ⚠️ Always ensure your payment is confirmed to unlock your full bidding potential.

---

## 4. Auction Timing

- **Start:** End of May  
- **End:** Early June (~2 weeks total)  
- **Team Timers:** Each team has its own countdown timer.
